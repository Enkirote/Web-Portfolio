UI/UX Design Figma Mobile & Web Case Study

Activity Bridge

A platform connecting parents with after-school activity providers, making it easy for families to discover, enroll, and manage activities for their children.

My Role UI/UX Designer
Tools Figma
Platform Mobile & Web
Type Concept Project
Overview

The Problem

Parents struggle to find, compare, and enroll their children in after-school activities. Activity providers lack a central platform to reach interested families efficiently.

The Solution

Activity Bridge connects both sides — parents can browse and enroll in activities, while providers can list, manage, and track enrollments in one place.

Target Users

Parents looking for structured, safe after-school activities for their children, and activity providers wanting to grow their reach and manage enrollments.

My Approach

Designed end-to-end in Figma — from mapping user flows and information architecture to building wireframes and applying the final visual design system.
