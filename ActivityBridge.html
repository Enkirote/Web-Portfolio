Overview

The Problem
🔍

Discovery Gap

Parents struggle to find quality after-school activities for their children. Information is scattered across websites, flyers, and word of mouth.

📋

Enrollment Friction

Signing up for activities is often manual and time-consuming, involving phone calls, paper forms, and back-and-forth communication.

📊

Provider Visibility

Activity providers lack a central platform to manage enrollments, communicate with families, and grow their programs effectively.
